Our Services

Acupuncture for pain relief

We relieve aches and pains including back pain, headaches, arthritis and more. So, If you've tried medication, ice packs and hot packs and still found yourself suffering, acupuncture may be exactly what you're looking for

Acupuncture for fertility

If you are planning to start a family, acupuncture can help manage any underlying health issues and support your overall health and well-being which in turn optimises your chances of successful conception.

Acupuncture for anxiety

Acupuncture can help relieve stress by regulating neurotransmitters in the brain associated with stress and pain. It can also restore harmony in your body by restoring the natural balance of your mental, physical and emotional health.

Stop smoking with acupuncture

We know it's not easy to stop smoking but acupuncture can help with building your will power and eventually eradicating your smoking addiction. With more than 80% success rate, why don't you give it a try?

Acupuncture for digestive issues

We can help you relieve IBS and other digestive issues. We use an all natural approach to relieving your symptoms without having to give up healthy and tasty foods. Book your acupuncture sessions with us in York.

Acupuncture in pregnancy

Acupuncture is ideal for expecting mothers. From morning sickness, breech and posterior babies, preparing for labour and postnatal care. We can support you throughout your pregnancy journey from our practice in York.
